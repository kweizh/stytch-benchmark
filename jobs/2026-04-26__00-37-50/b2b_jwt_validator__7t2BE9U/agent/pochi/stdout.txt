### Validation logic
- `stytch.B2BClient` is instantiated once at startup with `STYTCH_B2B_PROJECT_ID` / `STYTCH_B2B_SECRET`.
- `stytchClient.sessions.authenticateJwtLocal({ session_jwt })` performs **pure local verification**: the SDK fetches the Stytch JWKS on first use, caches it, and then validates every subsequent JWT cryptographically without any outbound network call to Stytch's authenticate endpoint.
