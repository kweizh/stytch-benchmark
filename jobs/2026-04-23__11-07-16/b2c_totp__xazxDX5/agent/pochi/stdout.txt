- `POST /users` calls `client.users.create({ email })` and returns `user_id`.
- `POST /totp/enroll` calls `client.totps.create({ user_id })` and returns `totp_id` + `secret` (the base32 secret already provided by Stytch in that field).
- `POST /totp/verify` calls `client.totps.authenticate({ user_id, totp_code, session_duration_minutes: 60 })` and returns `session_token`.
